Affordable Child Care in Hendersonville, TN

If you are seeking affordable child care in the area of Hendersonville, TN, look no further. Kids World Academy provides high quality, affordable child care to local families and has a solid reputation for exceptional care. Parents want to know that their children are safe, secure, and loved while attending a day care. The staff at Kids World Academy are some of the finest child care providers in the state.

Kids World Academy not only provides a safe and loving environment, but it thrives on being a learning center where kids can learn and grow in many ways. The professional teaching staff takes pride in fostering structured and fun environments so that infants and children can thrive. In fact, at just about any moment you will find teachers engaging in play and conversation with children one on one and in groups.

Infants

Infants receive exceptional care where they can receive abundant love and care through music, blanket time, cuddling, and exercise. We understand that infants need an atmosphere filled with love, peace, and positivity. This is why we hire the best of the best nursery workers in the state.

Toddlers

Toddlers have plenty of room to play and learn. They hear many entertaining and educational stories, learn and sing fun songs, go outside and play, and play with all sorts of toys. We want toddlers to feel free to explore safely and securely.

Preschoolers

Kids World offers a preschool program so that 4 and 5 year olds can be geared up and ready for Kindergarten educationally, emotionally, and socially. They will learn in areas of science, math, language, and music, which will give them a great head start as they head off to school. The facility also has a large playground where kids can laugh, run, and play with their peers.

Affordability

It is important to be able to afford high quality child care and that is why we make our services quite affordable for every family. We have parents thank us continually for making it possible to bring their children to a top-notch child care facility for such affordable prices. There really should be no reason why infants and children cannot receive high quality care regardless of their financial situation.

We care about you and your children and that is why we provide such invaluable services for affordable prices. Child care is more than just a business; it is an opportunity team up with parents to mold and shape a childs future. That is priceless!
