Welcome to Kids World Academy, a quality learning center for children from 6 weeks to 12 years of age located in Hendersonville, TN.

Kids World Academy is a truly new, innovative, and affordable concept in childcare and preschool education, offering the finest nursery facilities available for children 6 weeks to 24 months, and a full day/year-round professional teaching staff for preschoolers ages 2 to 5 years.

Our preschool program is designed for 4 to 5 year olds to prepare them for their all important school years ahead, and we offer an extensive school-age program for before and after school, including transportation to and from area public schools and planned activities to keep them busy.

Open M-F from 6:30 am – 6:00 pm

The Environment

Kids World Academy provides a clean, safe and educational environment for children and their families. In each classroom, children develop skills that promote physical, emotional, social and intellectual growth. We believe in being supportive of our parents, and we welcome their positive involvement at Kids World Academy. We further believe that within our environment, children can thrive and have the opportunity to blossom into responsible and contributing members of our society.

Our Amazing Staff

The Kids World Academy staff are courteous, professional and they love kids. We require on-going training for our staff through continuing education classes administered by the state of TN, orientation videos, and in-house training. All of our full-time staff are trained in First Aid and CPR. To ensure a safe and nurturing environment, all Kids World Academy employees have undergone a background check and fingerprinting by the state.

Services

Infants

Our infant suite provides the highest quality care in an environment that is designed with the parent and child in mind. While in our care, the children are stimulated through music, exercise, blanket time, and facial and verbal mimicking.

Toddlers

At this age, children are meeting new challenges as fast as they come. Crawling, walking, and eventually running are levels that they accomplish in what seems like a blink of an eye, and children learn best if they are in a stimulating environment. Music, stories, outdoor play, and center play envelop much of the day. Our caregivers are dedicated to helping each child develop skills through a trusting and loving relationship. A parental daily report is given to update each family on meal times, nap times, toilet training and all activities accomplished each day.

Preschoolers

A good preschool can make a difference in a child’s future school years, and Kids World Academy offers programs for children to help them learn in an environment that is stress free and fun. Preschoolers are able to explore a variety of learning centers which include music, science, math, language arts, manipulative play, and are also able to enjoy outdoor play utilizing the Kid’s World Academy playground facilities.

Policies

  1. Daycare hours are 6:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
  2. Tuition is to be paid in advance every Monday. There will be a fee of $20 for payments made after Tuesday 9:00 am.
  3. There will be a fee of $1/minute for children picked up after 6:00 pm.
  4. There is an annual non-refundable registration fee of $75 per family.
  5. Charges will still accumulate if your child(ren) is/are absent anytime during the week including any planned vacations by you (the parent), and holidays that fall on weekdays. This assures your child’s permanent slot, at the daycare, will not be filled.
  6. We provide Breakfast, Lunch, and an afternoon snack.
  7. It is the daycare’s responsibility to report ANY signs of child abuse or neglect of a child, at any given time.
